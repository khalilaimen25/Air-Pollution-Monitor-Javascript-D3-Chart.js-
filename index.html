Air pollution monitor

Air Pollution Monitor provides current, forecast and historical air pollution plus weather data for any coordinates on the globe
Besides basic Air Quality Index, the Monitor returns data about polluting gases, such as Carbon monoxide (CO), Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Ozone (O3), Sulphur dioxide (SO2), Ammonia (NH3), and particulates (PM2.5 and PM10).
